Display support tickets

Perform this task to display support ticket summary.

Procedure

From the left navigation pane, select Workflows > Support Tickets.

Parameters

  • Username: Username of the terminal user that has sent the support ticket.

  • Title: Title of the support ticket.

  • Content: Content of the support ticket.

  • State: State of the support ticket.

  • Sent At: Time at which the terminal user sent the support ticket.

  • Action: Actions that operators can perform on the support ticket.

  • To process the support ticket, click the  icon.

  • To display the support ticket, click the  icon.
