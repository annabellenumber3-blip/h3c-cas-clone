Process a support ticket

Perform this task to reply to the questions, suggestions, and remarks submitted by users.

Procedure

  1. From the left navigation pane, select Workflows > Support Tickets.

  1. Click the  icon in the Action column for the target support ticket.

  1. Enter the comment, and then click Send.

Parameters

  • Username: Username of the terminal user that has sent the support ticket.

  • Title: Title of the support ticket.

  • Content: Content of the support ticket.

  • Comment: Content of the message that the operator has sent to reply to the support ticket.
