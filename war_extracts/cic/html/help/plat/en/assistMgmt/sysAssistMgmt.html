Assistant management

This feature allows you to set the skin for the Web interface, log off an operator, and display version information.

Features

  1. Set the skin for the Web interface

  1. Click the  icon in the upper right corner of the homepage.

  1. Log off an operator

  1. Click the  icon in the upper right corner of the homepage.

  1. To use CIC, a logged-off operator must re-log in to the system.

  1. Display version information

  1. Click the  icon in the upper right corner of the homepage, and then select About.

  1. To close the dialog box, click Close.

  1. Use the online help

  1. Click the  icon in the upper right corner of the homepage, and then select Help.

  1. Access the technology forum

  1. Click the  icon in the upper right corner of the homepage, and then select Tech Forum.

  1. Download documents

  1. Click the  icon in the upper right corner of the homepage, and then select Download.
