Delete a user group or subgroup

Perform this task to delete a user group or subgroup. A user group that contains subgroups cannot be deleted.

Restrictions and guidelines

To delete a user group that contains subgroups, you must delete the subgroups first.

Procedure

  1. From the left navigation pane, select VDC > Users & Groups > User Groups.

  1. For a parent group, click the  icon in the Action column. For a subgroup, click the  icon to the left of its parent group name, and then click the  icon in the Action column.

  1. Click OK.
