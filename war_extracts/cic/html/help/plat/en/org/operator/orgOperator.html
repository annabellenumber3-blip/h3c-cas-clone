Manage administrators and administrator groups of an organization

In CIC, an operator can manage the administrators and administrator groups of an organization if the operator has the right to manage the organization and to manage operators and operator groups in system management. If an operator has the right to manage an organization but does not have the right to manage operators and operator groups in system management, the operator can only view administrators and administrator groups of the organization.

Features

  • Display administrators and administrator groups of an organization

  • Add an administrator for an organization

  • Add an administrator group for an organization

  • Delete an administrator or administrator group for an organization
