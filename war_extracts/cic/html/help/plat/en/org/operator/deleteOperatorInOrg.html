Delete an administrator or administrator group for an organization

Perform this task to delete an administrator's or administrator group's right to manage an organization.

Restrictions and guidelines

This task only deletes an administrator's right to manage an organization. The administrator is not deleted from CIC.

Procedure

  1. From the left navigation pane, select VDC > Organizations > Organization name.

  1. Click the Organization Administrators tab.

  1. Click the  icon in the Action column for an administrator or administrator group.

  1. Click OK.
