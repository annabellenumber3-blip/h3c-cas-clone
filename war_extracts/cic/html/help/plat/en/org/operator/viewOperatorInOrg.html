Display administrators and administrator groups of an organization

Perform this task to display administrators and administrator groups of an organization.

Procedure

  1. From the left navigation pane, select VDC > Organizations > Organization name.

  1. Click the Organization Administrators tab.

Parameters

  • Login Name/Group Name: Administrator login name or administrator group name.

  • Type: Whether the item is for an administrator or administrator group. In an administrator group, all administrators that have the management right to the organization can manage the organization.

  • Action: Actions that operators can perform on the administrator or administrator group. To delete the administrator, click the  icon.
