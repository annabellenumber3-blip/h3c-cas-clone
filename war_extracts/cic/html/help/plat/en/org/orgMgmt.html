Manage organizations  

Organizations are virtual data centers formed by virtual resource pools based on physical platforms. An organization contains logical resources, terminal users, VMs, VM templates, organization administrators, and organization management policies. An organization can be a department in the private cloud of an enterprise or an external customer of a service provider's public cloud.

Features

  • Display organizations

  • Add an organization

  • Modify an organization

  • Delete an organization

  • Display organization summary

  • Manage VMs of an organization

  • Manage VM templates of an organization

  • Manage resource pools of an organization

  • Manage users of an organization

  • Manage user groups of an organization

  • Manage administrators and administrator groups of an organization
