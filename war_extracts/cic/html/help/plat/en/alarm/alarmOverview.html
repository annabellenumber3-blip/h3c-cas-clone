Display alarm summary

Perform this task to display information about alarms within a specific time range.

Procedure

From the left navigation pane, select Alarms.

Parameters

  • Total Alarms for Past 7 Days: Total number of alarms of the past seven days for different cloud resources. The horizontal axis represents time, and the vertical axis represents the number of alarms. To display total alarms over the past seven days for a cloud resource, click the resource name.

  • Top 5 Hosts by Unconfirmed Alarms: Names and bar chart for the top 5 hosts with the greatest number of unconfirmed alarms. The horizontal axis represents name of the cloud resource, and the vertical axis represents the number of alarms.

  • Alarm Level Statistics: Pie chart for the numbers of alarms of different levels to the total number of alarms in percentage. To display alarm level statistics of a specific day, click .
