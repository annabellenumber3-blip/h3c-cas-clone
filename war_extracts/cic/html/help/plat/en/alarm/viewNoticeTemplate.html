Display alarm notification templates

Perform this task to display information about all alarm notification templates in CIC.

Procedure

From the left navigation pane, select Alarms > Notification Templates.

Parameters

  • Type: Alarm notification type, including mail notification and SMS notification.

  • Destination Address: Destination address of the alarm notification.

  • Alarm Level: Alarm level.

  • Alarm Rule: Alarm rule.

  • Action: Actions that operators can perform on the alarm notification template.

  • To modify the alarm notification template, click the  icon.

  • To delete the alarm notification template, click the  icon.
