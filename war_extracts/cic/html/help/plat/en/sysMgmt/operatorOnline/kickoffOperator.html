Log off an operator

Perform this task to log off an operator. To use CIC, a logged-off operator must re-log in to the system.

Restrictions and guidelines

To log off another operator, an operator must be authorized.

Procedure

  1. From the left navigation pane, select System > Operators & Groups > Online Operators.

  1. Select the target operators, and then click Log Off Now.

  1. In the dialog box that opens, click OK.
